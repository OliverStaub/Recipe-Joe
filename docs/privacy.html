Introduction

RecipeJoe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address - Used for account creation and authentication
  • Name (optional) - If provided during Sign in with Apple

User Content

  • Recipes - Recipe names, ingredients, steps, and notes you create or import
  • Photos - Recipe images you upload or capture

Technical Information

  • Device identifier - Used to associate your data with your account
  • App preferences - Your language and display settings

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the RecipeJoe service
  • Authenticate your account
  • Store and sync your recipes across devices
  • Process recipe imports using AI (text extraction from URLs, images, and PDFs)
  • Improve our service

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

Supabase

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. Your account information and recipes are stored securely on Supabase servers. Supabase Privacy Policy

Anthropic (Claude AI)

When you import recipes from images, PDFs, or websites, we use Anthropic's Claude AI to extract recipe information. The content you import is processed by Claude but is not used to train AI models. Anthropic Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security
  • Only you can access your recipes and account data
  • We use HTTPS for all data transmission
  • Recipe images are stored securely in cloud storage

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data (including recipes, images, and personal information) is permanently deleted from our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - View all data associated with your account
  • Export - Export your recipes
  • Delete - Delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app (Settings > Delete Account)
  • Correct - Update or correct your information at any time

Children's Privacy

RecipeJoe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
